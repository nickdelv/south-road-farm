Pricing & Availability

Weekends at South Road Farm start at $12,000. That's a single, all-in price — no à la carte fees, no vendor surcharges, no hidden add-ons. You get the full property: both barns, the farmhouse, all ceremony sites, and every outdoor space from Friday afternoon through Sunday midday. Pricing varies by weekend, with peak dates in September and October priced higher. Reach out and we'll send a full breakdown.
We book weddings May through October. We intentionally keep the calendar small — a limited number of weekends per season — so that every event gets our full attention. If you have a date in mind, reach out early. Popular weekends, particularly in September and October, tend to go first.
The property is yours from Friday afternoon through Sunday at noon. That includes time to arrive, set up, rehearse, get ready, celebrate, and wind down — without feeling rushed at any point. Sunday morning is part of every booking, not an add-on.
Reach out through our contact form or email us at hello@southroad.farm. We'll follow up with availability and next steps. A signed agreement and deposit officially hold your date.

The Property

South Road Farm comfortably hosts up to 150 guests. There's ample space for dining, dancing, and gathering both indoors and out — across two barns, multiple decks, the courtyard, and the surrounding grounds.
The farmhouse is included in every weekend rental — no separate fee. It sleeps 8–10 comfortably across 4 bedrooms and 2.5 bathrooms. It's perfect for the wedding party, immediate family, or out-of-town guests who want to be on-site. You decide who stays there.

Day Of

Per town ordinance, amplified music ends by 11:00 pm and all non-overnight guests depart by 11:30 pm. After that, anyone staying in the farmhouse is welcome to keep the evening going at their own pace — the fire pit is yours, the kitchen is yours, the night is yours.
