The Experience

Not a venue.
A gathering.

South Road Farm isn't built for weddings in the traditional sense. No banquet hall, no required caterer, no schedule laminated to the wall. Two restored barns, 66 acres of open sky, a farmhouse that sleeps your people, and three days that are entirely yours.

The energy builds from Friday evening welcome drinks to a ceremony under open skies, late-night dancing, food trucks, and firelight — and eases into Sunday morning with coffee and the best kind of slow. You shape it. We designed the space to let that happen.

Our Story

It started with
a wedding arch.

In 2022, Nick's brother-in-law Dave — a lifelong carpenter — built a wooden arch for Nick's own wedding. That arch still stands on the ceremony lawn. Dave has spent the years since restoring nearly everything you see here: both barns, the farmhouse, every deck. By hand, largely himself. This place has the feeling it has because of how it was made.
