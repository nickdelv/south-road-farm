South Road Farm  ·  Fayette, Maine

The
Weekend.

From a ceremony under open skies to late-night dancing and Sunday morning coffee — this is what a weekend here looks like.

The Arc

Built to
unfold.

No single room. No rigid timeline. The celebration builds across spaces and moments — a ceremony with panoramic views or tucked into the trees, cocktail hour with live music and firelight, dinner under the string lights, and a dance floor that doesn't quit until the music does.

Every element is yours to shape. We designed the setting to make that feel effortless.

Saturday

The
Ceremony.

South Road Farm has multiple ceremony sites — an open-field setting with bold, panoramic views in every direction, and a wooded site nestled into the trees for something more intimate. A wooden arch, rows of wooden benches, and land that does most of the heavy lifting.

After the Ceremony

Cocktail
Hour.

The ceremony ends and everyone exhales. The bar opens, live music fills the field, and guests drift between the fire pits, the lawn, and the courtyard. Golden hour takes care of the rest.

Dinner + Drinks

Food trucks.
Firelight.

As the sun drops, the food trucks line up and the whole evening shifts gear. You bring whatever fits your crew — we have the power hookups, the courtyard, and the space to make it a proper scene.

The Reception

Pie.
Dancing.
All of it.

The barn fills up. Jackets come off. The dance floor doesn't stop. Somewhere along the way there's a dessert table, a coffee cart by the door, and a pie that disappears faster than expected.

Late Night

The part
nobody forgets.

The barn lights low. String lights out front. Someone swapped their shoes hours ago. The food window is still glowing. The party finds its own pace — because we built the space to let it.

This is the part you'll be texting each other about for years.
